About Us

Canadian Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Ltd. is a refrigeration and air conditioning service company located at A3 - 5279 Still Creek Avenue, Burnaby BC, V5C 5V1. We provide services 24/7.

Established in August 1979, we are dedicated to providing top-notch HVAC services in Metro Vancouver Area, including Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Coquitlam, Langley and Surrey.
