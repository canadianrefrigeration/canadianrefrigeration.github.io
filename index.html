About Us

Canadian Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Ltd. was established in August 1979. Our Office, Warehouse, Show Room and Shop are conveniently located in Burnaby, British Columbia. Over the years we have developed a long list of loyal clienteles ranging from Hospitals under Providence Health Care, Colleges, GreenTee Country Club and number of reputable Hotels and Restaurants in the Lower Mainland. Our motto is “Better service through knowledge.” We have worked hard to build trust and customer satisfaction. The fact that we have maintained a lasting relationship with our clients is a testament to our commitment and professionalism. We fully recognize that the business we are in requires promptness, professionalism, and experience. We provide all. Our key personnel are required to maintain currency of knowledge about the latest equipment by attending the relevant courses.

What We Do

Our primary area of expertise includes Walk In Coolers and Freezers, Refrigeration of all types, Roof top Air Conditioners, Ice Machines, Complete Installation, Preventative Maintenance, Parts and related equipment supply.

Our Rates and Operation Schedule

Our charge out rate is a very competitive $120.00 an hour for regular time. This is considerably lower than the industry standard. 10% special labour discount is given to regular customers.

Our regular hours are 7:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Monday through Friday and, from 8:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. on Saturday. We are a 24 hours and 7 days a week service company. Outside of regular hours, we are on call at any time. All work outside of regular hours is charged at “time and a half” with a two-hour minimum call out charge. There is no overtime charge if a job is commenced during regular hours till job is completed. All calls carry a $75.00 van charge (15 miles radius). Our response time is usually one to three hours depending on the time of day and traffic. Our emergency response time is one hour. Regular charge out per service call is two hours minimum. Our Shop rate is $120.00 an hour. Above rates are valid for one year from the day of acceptance. Same day service guarantee if the call the placed during the early hours of the day.

Warranty

Our services are covered by a one-year manufacturers' warranty on parts and, a ninety-day Technical Services warranty.

Liability Insurance and WCB

We carry liability insurance on all our services. However, we are proud to say that in over thirty years of service, we have not had a single claim against us. We are covered with Work Safe BC.
