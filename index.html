About Us

Established in August 1979, Canadian Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Ltd. is dedicated to providing top-notch HVAC services in Metro Vancouver Area, mainly Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Coquitlam and Surrey.

Commitment

What We Do

We specialize in:

  • Walk-In, Reach-in, Under-counter Coolers / Freezers
  • Refrigeration of all types
  • Roof top air conditioning / heating units
  • Ice / water machines
  • Complete Installation, Preventative Maintenance, Parts, and related equipment supply
